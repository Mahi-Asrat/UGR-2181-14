🌍 My Volunteer Experience

Summer Teacher (2016-2017)

In the summer after my sophomore year at high school, I was able to teach the 5 core subjects at John F. Kenndey Elementary School. I also used to tutor kids after class and I received a certificate of Appreciation for that.

Assistant Librarian (2014 - Present)

At the Kolfe Keraniyo sub-city library, I was able to collect and organize books while assisting library users check out books. One of my greatest achievements there was organizing book donation events where several renowned artists and authors joined.

Planting Campaign (Lock-down)

I was able to participate in a planting campaign organized by Interact Club of Cathe's Vega during quarantine.

President of LCCS's STEM Club (2018-2021)

During my service there, I led the club by motivating girls to be participants in the STEM field. In collaboration with Col. John C. Robinson of the American center, we also used to engage in several contests and group projects.
